About me

Hello there! I'm a college student currently pursuing my passion at the State Polytechnic of Malang. My academic journey revolves around the world of Computer Science, where I'm constantly exploring the exciting realms of technology and programming.

Beyond the lines of code and algorithms, I'm also a freelance artist. I find immense joy in the creative process, and my canvas often extends from digital screens to paper and canvas, bringing my imagination to life through drawings and illustrations.

One of my unique interests is mechanical keyboards. There's something oddly satisfying about the click-clack of mechanical switches and the customizability they offer. As a mechanical keyboard enthusiast, I love tinkering with different switches, keycap sets, and layouts to create the perfect typing experience.
